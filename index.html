About 


      Barnabas "Barney" Stinson is a fictional character portrayed by Neil Patrick Harris and created for the CBS television series How I Met Your Mother.
      He is known for his love of wearing expensive suits, laser tag, alcohol and various catchphrases.
      Oftenly says: "Suit up!", "Legendary", "Wait for it" (often combining the two as "legen—wait for it—dary!").
      In later seasons, he has a few serious relationships, marries, divorces, and has a child.
